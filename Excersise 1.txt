Writing an instruction to find the subway

Get out through the classroom door
Turn to your left 
Take the first right to locate elevator
Press No 1 in the elevator to lobby
turn to your left.
Turn to your left again
Leave the building through the door infront of you
Turn to the right
Turn to the right again at the first turn to go by the side of the same building
At the end of the building block, cross the street
You are at your destination.
